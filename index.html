Set against the backdrop of World War II, "The Boy and the Heron" tells the heartwarming story of Mahito, a young boy who seeks solace in the tranquility of the countryside after losing his mother in a bombing raid. There, he moves with his father to a rural area, where he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and emotional healing.
